M.A Lunches


Welcome to M.A Lunches, the company with the most delicious, healthy food. A single bite of our puff pastry pizza will make you crave for more and our other meals will make your mouth water. Founded by Aditya Nair and Mayank Medhekar, their company made a promise to be the most healthiest vegetarian company in the world. Check out their tasty foods they have created.

Puff Pastry Pizza

    Try our newest lunchbox meal, puff pastry pizza. 1 bite will make you want to eat more. It includes unsaturated fats, no salt and only includes vegetables and no meat.

Apple Oatmeal Cinnamon Biscuits

    Inspired by the incredible master chef, Mrs Myers, the apple oatmeal cinnamon biscuits are the most tastiest biscuits you have ever tried. It includes apple, oats and only unsaturated fats.

Carrot Muffins

    Again inspired by Mrs Myers, the carrot muffins will be the only food you will crave on the weekend. Including carrots, marmalade and cinnamon, this will be the healthiest muffin you have ever tasted.

Bread Sushi

    Invented by the great chef Mrs Myers, she used only her surroundings to make one of the most healthiest, tastiest lunches ever made. This incredible food does not include a single saturated fat but only bread, cucumber, carrot and eggless mayonese! It's quite impressive how a few ingredients make somethign so tasty.
